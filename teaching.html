Teaching

I am passionate about teaching and have taught undergraduate or masters level courses during most of the semesters I have been at Berkeley.

The undergraduate classes I have TA'ed for include Industrial Organization (4x), Intermediate Microeconomics (1x), Health Economics (2x) and Psychology and Economics (1x). I received an Outstanding Graduate Student Instructor Award in 2019. I have taught in person and both synchronously and asynchronously online.

I have also taught Data Analytics and Introductory Machine Learning and Causal Inference to MBA students at Haas (3x). I developed homeworks and labs in R as well as quizzes and was involved in preparing and developing the course to transition from an elective to a core course.
